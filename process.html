The Lifecycle

Four Phases.
One Direction.

Phase 01 — Consultation

The Foundation.

Define intent before materials. Observational

We sit across the table. I need to see the raw vision — not the polished pitch. Intent gets defined before a single resource is committed.

  • Complete The Foundation Brief
  • Stakeholder & vision alignment
  • Problem definition & scope read

Phase 02 — Proposal

The Blueprint.

Geometry meets vision. Technical

A clear, formatted plan — timeline, resources, milestones, and a definition of "done" we both hold each other to.

  • Full scope documentation
  • Timeline & milestone mapping
  • Success metrics definition

Phase 03 — Development

The Construction.

Ideas become real. Collaborative

We build the engine, refine the texture, test the weight. Iterative cycles, every decision oriented toward what we defined in Phase 02.

  • Iterative build cycles & check-ins
  • Vendor & team coordination
  • QA & stress-testing

Phase 04 — Launch

The Open House.

Keys are yours. Empowered

You step into a finished space — supported, documented, and built to scale. The project graduates into its next chapter.

  • Full launch execution
  • Operational handoff & docs
  • Retrospective & growth roadmap
